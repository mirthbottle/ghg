Except as noted below, all CAIT data carries a Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial 4.0 International license. Data sources	

CAIT 2.0 UNFCCC data derives directly from United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change (UNFCCC) Secretariat	
	
Additionally to citing CAIT 2.0 please consider citing  UNFCCC data source that CAIT 2.0 is using:	
		
United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change (UNFCCC) Secretariat. 2013. "Time Series – Annex I." Bonn: UNFCCC. Available at: http://unfccc.int/ghg_data/ghg_data_unfccc/time_series_annex_i/items/3814.php	
	

Additional information for specific country sources can be found at: http://unfccc.int/ghg_data/ghg_data_unfccc/data_sources/items/3816.php
